About the Project

About Delhi's Mausoleum Project

A digital humanities initiative documenting Delhi's inscriptions, monuments, and scripts through interactive mapping and archival research.

Disclaimer

Project Disclaimer

The project was done in the Cluster Innovation Centre by students of Semester 4th under the supervision of Professor Dr. Ashu Mishra. This initiative combines historical research and digital technologies to map and create an accessible archive of Delhi's Mausoleum.

About Cluster Innovation Centre

The Cluster Innovation Center, or CIC for short, was created to look for and extract innovations from slum, industrial, village, and educational clusters. It is an attempt to use the enthusiasm and commitment of intelligent young people to address some of India's and civil society's most pressing issues.
